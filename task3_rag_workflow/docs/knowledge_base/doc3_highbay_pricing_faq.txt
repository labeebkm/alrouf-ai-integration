DOCUMENT: AL ROUF LED High Bay Light Series — Product Specification
SKU: LED-HIGHBAY-150W | Category: Industrial / High Bay

Overview
--------
The AL ROUF LED High Bay Light (150W) targets warehouses, factories, gymnasiums,
and large retail halls with ceiling heights between 6 m and 15 m.
It replaces 400W MH/HPS high-bay fixtures at significant energy savings.

Technical Specifications
------------------------
- Wattage: 150W (±5%)
- Luminous Flux: 19,500 lm
- Luminous Efficacy: 130 lm/W
- Colour Temperature: 4000K / 5700K
- CRI: ≥80
- Beam Angle: 60° / 90° / 120° (specify on order)
- Input Voltage: 100–277V AC, 50/60Hz
- Power Factor: ≥0.95
- THD: <10%
- Surge Protection: 6kV
- Dimensions: Ø385×210 mm (round UFO style)
- Weight: 4.5 kg
- IP Rating: IP65
- IK Rating: IK08
- Operating Temperature: -30°C to +50°C
- Lifespan: ≥80,000 hours (L70)
- Warranty: 5 years

Certifications
--------------
CE, RoHS, CB, TUV, SAA (optional), DLC Premium (optional for North America)

Mounting Options
----------------
- Hook mount (standard — 1.5 m hook included)
- Pendant rod (optional — specify length)
- Surface/flange mount bracket (optional)
- Yoke bracket for wall/beam mounting (optional)

Dimming
-------
Compatible with 0-10V dimming and DALI-2. Integrated motion sensor option available (+15W standby).

Applications
------------
Warehouses, distribution centres, manufacturing plants, gymnasiums, supermarkets.

DOCUMENT: AL ROUF Pricing & Payment FAQ

Q: What is the pricing structure?
A: AL ROUF uses tiered volume pricing. Unit prices decrease at standard breakpoints of 50, 100,
500, 1,000, and 5,000 units. Exact pricing is provided in formal quotations valid for 30 days.

Q: What currencies does AL ROUF accept?
A: USD is the primary trading currency. EUR, GBP, and AED are also accepted for orders over
USD 10,000 with prior agreement.

Q: What payment terms are available?
A: Standard terms are 30% advance payment with purchase order confirmation, and 70% balance
before shipment. Letter of Credit (LC at sight) is available for orders over USD 50,000.
Established distributors may negotiate 30-day net terms after 6 months of trading history.

Q: Are samples available?
A: Yes. Pre-production samples are available at standard unit price plus courier cost.
Sample lead time is 3–5 business days for standard products.

Q: Are OEM / private label services offered?
A: Yes. AL ROUF offers OEM packaging, custom colour boxes, firmware branding for smart
products, and printed labels. MOQ for OEM branding is 500 units. Lead time +7 business days.

Q: Is there a distributor programme?
A: Yes. AL ROUF operates a tiered distributor programme (Silver / Gold / Platinum) with
exclusive territory options, marketing support, and enhanced warranty pass-through.
Contact sales@alrouf.com for the distributor application form.
