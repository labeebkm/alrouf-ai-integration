DOCUMENT: AL ROUF LED Panel Light Series — Product Specification
SKU: LED-PANEL-60W | Category: Indoor Commercial

Overview
--------
The AL ROUF LED Panel Light (60W) is a premium indoor luminaire designed for office, retail,
and commercial environments. It delivers 6,600 lumens with a colour rendering index (CRI) of ≥90,
providing accurate colour representation and visual comfort.

Technical Specifications
------------------------
- Wattage: 60W (±5%)
- Luminous Flux: 6,600 lm
- Luminous Efficacy: 110 lm/W
- Colour Temperature: 3000K / 4000K / 6500K (specify on order)
- CRI: ≥90
- Beam Angle: 120°
- Input Voltage: 100–277V AC, 50/60Hz
- Power Factor: ≥0.95
- THD: <10%
- Dimensions: 600×600×12 mm
- Weight: 2.8 kg
- IP Rating: IP40 (indoor use)
- IK Rating: IK06
- Operating Temperature: -20°C to +45°C
- Lifespan: ≥50,000 hours (L70)
- Warranty: 5 years

Certifications
--------------
CE, RoHS, CB, SAA (Australia), DLC (North America option)

Installation
------------
Recessed mounting into standard 600×600 mm suspended ceiling grid.
Compatible with DALI, 0-10V, and PWM dimming systems.
Minimum ceiling height: 2.5 m.

Applications
------------
Open-plan offices, retail stores, schools, hospitals, hotel lobbies.

DOCUMENT: AL ROUF LED Street Light Series — Product Specification
SKU: LED-STREET-100W | Category: Outdoor / Street Lighting

Overview
--------
The AL ROUF LED Street Light (100W) is engineered for roadway and area illumination.
It replaces 250W HPS/MH fixtures while delivering superior photometric performance.

Technical Specifications
------------------------
- Wattage: 100W (±5%)
- Luminous Flux: 13,000 lm
- Luminous Efficacy: 130 lm/W
- Colour Temperature: 4000K / 5700K
- CRI: ≥70
- Beam Optics: Type II / Type III (configurable)
- Input Voltage: 100–277V AC, 50/60Hz
- Power Factor: ≥0.95
- Surge Protection: 10kV
- Dimensions: 580×270×90 mm
- Weight: 5.2 kg
- IP Rating: IP66
- IK Rating: IK09
- Operating Temperature: -40°C to +55°C
- Lifespan: ≥100,000 hours (L70)
- Warranty: 7 years

Certifications
--------------
CE, RoHS, ENEC, UL (optional), IEC 62471 (photobiological safety)

Mounting
--------
Slip-fitter: 40–60 mm diameter poles.
Arm reach: 0–15° tilt adjustment.
Optional: 7-pin NEMA/ZHAGA socket for smart controls.

Smart Controls Compatibility
-----------------------------
Compatible with TALQ, DALI-2, and proprietary NB-IoT wireless dimming systems.
Supports individual and group scheduling, energy monitoring, and fault reporting.

Applications
------------
Urban roads, highways, parking lots, industrial areas, ports.
