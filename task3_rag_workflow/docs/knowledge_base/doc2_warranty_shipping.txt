DOCUMENT: AL ROUF Warranty & After-Sales Policy
Version: 2024-01

Standard Warranty Terms
------------------------
All AL ROUF LED products carry a standard manufacturer's warranty from the date of delivery:

| Product Category       | Warranty Period |
|------------------------|-----------------|
| Indoor Commercial      | 5 years         |
| Outdoor / Street       | 7 years         |
| High Bay / Industrial  | 5 years         |
| Decorative / Retail    | 3 years         |
| Smart Control Modules  | 2 years         |

Warranty Coverage
-----------------
The warranty covers manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship, including:
- Driver/power supply failures
- LED module lumen depreciation below L70 within rated lifespan
- Housing corrosion under normal operating conditions

The warranty does NOT cover:
- Physical damage, vandalism, or improper installation
- Damage from voltage spikes not within specified input range (unless surge-protected model)
- Products operated outside specified temperature range
- Unauthorised modifications or repairs

Claim Process
-------------
1. Contact AL ROUF support at warranty@alrouf.com with the order number, batch code, and description of fault.
2. AL ROUF will issue an RMA (Return Material Authorisation) number within 3 business days.
3. Return defective units freight prepaid to designated service centre.
4. AL ROUF will repair or replace at its discretion within 15 business days of receiving the RMA.

Replacement during warranty period is FOB Shenzhen. Outbound freight for replacements is covered by AL ROUF for orders over USD 5,000.

Extended Warranty
-----------------
Extended warranty (up to 10 years) available on request for projects over 500 units.
Contact your regional sales manager for terms.

DOCUMENT: AL ROUF Shipping & Lead Time Policy
Version: 2024-02

Standard Lead Times
--------------------
Lead times are counted from receipt of 30% advance payment and confirmed purchase order:

| Order Size            | Standard Lead Time |
|-----------------------|--------------------|
| < 100 units           | 7–10 business days |
| 100–500 units         | 15–20 business days|
| 500–2,000 units       | 25–30 business days|
| > 2,000 units         | 35–45 business days|

Custom colour temperatures, special optics, or OEM branding add 5–10 business days.

Incoterms
----------
Default shipping terms: FOB Shenzhen.
Other available Incoterms: EXW, CIF, CFR (negotiable for orders > USD 20,000).

Shipping Methods
----------------
- Sea freight (FCL/LCL): Recommended for orders > 200 units.
- Air freight: Available for urgent orders; additional charges apply.
- Express courier (DHL/FedEx): For samples and orders < 20 units.

Customs & Documentation
------------------------
AL ROUF provides: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin,
and test reports (CE/RoHS/CB) as standard. Additional certifications (UL, DLC, SAA)
available on request with 5 business days notice.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)
-----------------------------
Most products: 50 units per SKU per order.
LED tubes and downlights: 200 units per SKU.
Sample orders (pre-production): 1–5 units at standard unit price.
